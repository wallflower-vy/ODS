About Event

DIGITAL TECHNOLOGY-KEY TO SUSTAINABLE FUTURE

Ogun Digital Summit 2022 is an all-encompassing platform targeted to engage all tech enthusiasts ranging from entrepreneurs, creatives, freelancers, start-up founders, MDA’s, FinTech’s, students in and outside the state, corporate Bodies and the general public at large.

This is a day summit which will address and promote the digital technology/skills for the future and how these digital skills can be used to solve critical real-life problems in the near future. .

OUR MISSION, AIM AND SCOPE

We take priority in working on emerging technological trends to break uncertainties and shift the digital paradigm through practical technical knowledge.

Our aim is to equip everyone with essential knowledge, adequate guidance and mentorship to pursue expert insights that meet digital needs.
